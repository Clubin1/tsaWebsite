Why use electric cars

For years the idea of climate change and ecological damage has been looming over our heads. Our own usage of coal, nuclear, natural gas and renewable sources has damanged our ozone layer and planet. The idea of electrical cars surfaced in the 1840's however their extinguishable counter part was more popular. By choosing to drive an electrical car you are not only helping the enviornment, but also giving time for the ozone layer to repair itself. Without the use of gas for energy, CO2 emissions are also greatly lowered, helping keep the air quality clean. Overall electrical cars have the potential to help the overall wellness of humans and our enviornment, especially in higher CO2 emission countries.
