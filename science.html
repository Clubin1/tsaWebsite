Biomedical and Forensic Sciences

Olympic offers expierenced science teachers

Honors PLTW Principles of Biomedical Science

Dr Terry

Principles of Biomedical science teaches us how to apply physical and formal sciences in the health care field. It combines the field of both biology, and medicene to save the lives of animals and humans.

Honors PLTW Human Body Systems

Dr Terry

In Human Body Systems, the students will use data acquisition software to monitor body functions. They often play the role as the biomedical professor. Students explore the aspects of biology and the human body, they take it a step further.

Honors PLTW Medical Interventions

Ms Castor

Students will learn more about the medical field and use medicene to study how to prevent diseases, treat diseases and prolong lifespan.

Honors PLTW Biomedical Innovations

Ms Chy

Students use all the past knowledge they've recieved from the previous courses and brainstorm solutions to the worlds most pressing health problems. The problems they must solve range from public health to physiology
