What is CTE?

Career and technical education (CTE) is the practice of teaching specific career skills to students in middle school, high school, and post-secondary institutions. The completion of a CTE pathway offers students the chance to earn themselves a NAF diploma, displaying career level readiness. NAF diplomas help ensure that students are college, career, and future ready. At Olympic High School, many different pathways are offered to get kids ready for multiple fields such as, buisness, engineering and biomedical sciences.
