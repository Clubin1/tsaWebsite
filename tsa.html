Our TSA Chapter

The OHS TSA chapter was created by Cameron Ki-Ny. Our TSA chapter has had many issues with advisors, fundraising and start up. Started by a handful of middle school veterans, our first year lacked advisors, but it wasn't a task that we couldn't handle. We went to states and even though we fell sort of winning, our second year is looking a lot more promising. Our first year consisted of just some friends that did it in middle school and this year our numbers have tripled.

Our Mission statement & Motto

Our TSA chapter enhances personal academic achievments and prepares students to excell in a CTE and STEM related enviornment. TSA integrates extra cirriculars, activites, competitions and STEM related thinking to win competitons.
